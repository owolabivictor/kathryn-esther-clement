About Kathryn

Kathryn Esther Clement is an award-winning spoken word artist, author of Finding Your Path, purpose coach, and inspirational content creator. Through poetry, writing, and mentorship, she empowers people to embrace purpose, resilience, and authentic self-expression. Her work has inspired audiences across conferences, events, and digital platforms, leaving a lasting impact on lives around the world.
