About Kathryn

Kathryn Esther Clement is an award-winning spoken word artist, author of Finding Your Path, inspirational writer, content creator, and purpose coach. Through powerful storytelling and spoken word, she inspires people to discover purpose, embrace resilience, and live with intention.
Her work explores themes of identity, hope, love, healing, and purpose, leaving lasting impressions on audiences across Nigeria. Kathryn has performed at major conferences and events, sharing messages that encourage personal growth and transformation.
Beyond the stage, she creates inspiring content, mentors individuals in discovering their life's purpose, and passionately advocates for mental health awareness and social impact. Her excellence has earned her several prestigious awards, making her a respected voice in the spoken word and personal development space.
